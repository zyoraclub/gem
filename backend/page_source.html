All MSE service providers who have participated in any service bid after 3rd February are advised to update/re-verify their MSE status in GeM profile to continue availing MSE purchase preference benefits. GeM has discontinued requirement of Caution Money. Sellers may withdraw existing deposit by logging in to their seller account. GeM is intermittently experiencing delays in response for MSME services. MSME sellers are advised to verify/ re-verify their status during non-business hours until services run smoothly to avoid any Business disruptions/ inconvenience. A Gentle Reminder: It is reminded that the buyers who have pending or expired GeM orders including SERVICES Orders placed on or before 14 December 2023, and have not closed these orders, will not be able to procure on GeM after 31 march 2026 until the orders are closed using Partial/Complete Order Closure or updated through bulk payment. GeM sellers can now explore expanded market access through the Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC) — learn more at https://portal.ondc.org/sign-up. All existing and new eWaste category bidders are requested to note that a new e-Waste (V2) category is now available for all relevant auctions; please re-register/register between 17-12-2025 and 15-01-2026 with required certificates for verification to ensure uninterrupted participation Dear sellers, GeM takes strict actions against the errant sellers.Don’t indulge in Bid Rigging, Cartelisation, tinkering, bypassing or any type of collusion. In compliance to recent decision of the GST Council, Sellers are advised to revise MRP and offer prices on GeM as per applicable revised GST rates and rules, ensuring prices are inclusive of applicable GST . Buyer Organisation having their mail domain may request for the whitelisting of their domain to easy out the issue of gem buyer id creation. Buyer wish to whitelist the domain may write to GeM Buyer nodal officer for the concerned state/ ministry with an undertaking that the mail domain is owned by the buyer organization and they will ensure that email ids of users who have retired from the organization are regularly being deactivated by them and they will also notify GeM about any such retirement or deactivation of the users.
